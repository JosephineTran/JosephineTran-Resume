I am a 3rd year French major at University of California, Riverside. I graduated from Huntington Beach High School in 2017. I was a part of Key Club International, Amnesty International, and French club throughout my 4 years in highschool. In my last year of highschool, I became the co-president of the French club.

I decided to pursue a major in French because I found the course to be the most enjoyable throughout highschool. My dad's family is fluent and I began to learn in middle school so I decided to continue learning. I always felt that learning another language or being multilingual would broaden a person's opportunities in the future. Knowing more languages allows for a worldwide perspective in situations as well as for one to develop connections around the world.

In the future, I would like to venture into an occupation(s) relating to businesses in the global field. Using the knowledge I have with French currently, I want to be an asset in a company's global expansion. I'd like to be in positions where I could serve as a liaison or translator for a company's international affairs. I hope that with my background in community service, awareness of global social issues and French studies that I'll make an impact in the future field I work in.
